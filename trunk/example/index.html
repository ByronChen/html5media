HTML5 video for everyone!

Enables <video> tags in all major browsers.

Video tag demo

Depending on your web browser, the below video will be shown using either a HTML5 video player, or a Flowplayer fallback.

At the time of writing, the HTML5 player will be used in the Firefox, Safari, Chrome, Opera, Android and iPhone browsers.

The above video was embedded using a HTML5 <video> tag. This tag can be enabled in all major browsers by including a single javascript file in your document.

Find out how to use HTML5 video tags in all major browsers at the html5media project homepage.
