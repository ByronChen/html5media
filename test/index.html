HTML 5 media compatibility layer

Provides support for <video> tags in all major browsers.

Video player demonstration

Depending on your web browser, the below video will be shown using either a HTML5 video player, or a Flowplayer fallback.

At the time of writing, the HTML5 player will be shown in the Safari, Chrome, Android and iPhone browsers.

Find out how to use HTML5 video tags in all major browsers at the html5media project homepage.
